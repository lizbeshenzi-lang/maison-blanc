/ 001 — The approach

Skin is not a canvas. It is an organ.

Maison Blanc was founded on a frustration with the beauty industry's habit of selling feeling over function. We opened in 2018 with a different proposition: evidence-based skin care, delivered by practitioners who understand physiology, not just aesthetics.

Every treatment at Maison Blanc begins with an assessment. Every protocol is built around your specific skin — its type, its history, its goals. We do not offer packages because no two clients need the same thing. We offer consultations, then we build a plan.

/ 003 — The team

Practitioners, not therapists

Everyone at Maison Blanc holds a clinical qualification. We recruit from nursing, dermatology, and aesthetics medicine. We do not recruit from beauty schools. The distinction matters: our team understands the biology of the treatments they deliver, not just the protocol.

All new treatments are supervised by Dr. Helena Voss, our medical director, until full clinical sign-off is given. That is non-negotiable.

Book a consultation or treatment

New clients begin with a consultation. It takes sixty minutes and costs £95, redeemable against your first treatment. This is not optional — we need to assess your skin before prescribing any clinical treatment.

Returning clients may book treatments directly. If your skin has changed significantly since your last visit, we may ask to reassess before proceeding.

Cancellation policy
We require 48 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations are charged at 50% of the treatment fee. No-shows are charged in full. This allows us to offer cancelled slots to clients on our waiting list.
